Exhibition

The project was developed in collaboration with Chalmers University of Technology and Universeum, and exhibited on the 25th and 26th of October 2019 at Universeum, Gothenburg, Sweden. The theme of the project was ‘showing the invisible’ and the target group was kids between the ages of 12-16.
